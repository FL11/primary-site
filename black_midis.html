Why do I keep my MIDIs private?Close

Because why not? :smirk:

It has come to my attention that there are other users who steal parts of others' MIDIs, which include phrases, and some segments. There are also cases where people modify others' works without the original author's explicit consent. As a result, all of my works are kept private and only accessible to certain people as a precaution.
